On The Bag's Mission

Everyone in the golf world both loves and hates golf. If you're like us, you love it, but you don't commit all your time to it. So we got you! Think of On The Bag as your own personal online caddie. We'll keep you up to date with important stories, great videos from popular AND smaller creators, and can be a tool to discover new courses and playing opportunities. Currently, we're focused on New Jersey based courses, as well as resorts and professional courses.

Meet the Team

Sean Carney

Sean is the employee that started it all here at On The Bag. He started into golf young with his family, and at just 14 years old, started caddying at his local country club. Since then, Sean has been enamored with golf in his free time. Caddying wise, he holds a high count of acolades, being on the bag for both a Mens and Women's club champion, as well as the Member-Guest champions of his club. In 2024, his club participated in the caddie open qualifying, and he outscored everyone in his flight to earn an invitation to the U.S. Caddie Open.

Since competeing in Pinehurst, Sean has been busy developing this site as a safespace for golfers like himself. His vision for the space is to have it be where casual golf fans can come, catch up with the professional game, find some interesting content, and learn about the local courses.
